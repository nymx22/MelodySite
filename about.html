TianYun Chen

About

Tianyun Chen (b.2002) is a multimedia artist and independent publisher at spec collective in Brooklyn, NY. They graduated from Emerson College with a BFA degree in Media Arts Production in 2025. Combining analog photography, book making, archival footage, text, and various video-audio manipulation techniques, their work often explores themes of identity, trauma, and interpersonal relationships. Lately their art concerns topics of youth lifestyle and diasporic experiences.
